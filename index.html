M Y S K I L L S

I'm well versed in using Figma for both wireframing and prototyping projects. However I much prefer to wireframe here.

I use Figma to quickly and effectivly communicate my deseign ideas from wireframing, to mid and high fidelity protoyping.

I use Miro in the early stages of projects.

On this platform, I store the artifacts from the empathy phase, data synthesization, feature prioritization, user flow and more. Keepig it in Miro not only makes it easy to track each step I've done, but also allows others who may be working the project with me, to follow along and edit in real time.

Much like Figma, I use Adobe XD to wireframe and prototype. However I favor this platform for protoyping due to the increased cabalilities.

I prefer to use this platform to protoype. Unlike Figma, Adobe XD allows me to do far more complex micro interactions. On this platform, I can add vocal interactions and more.
